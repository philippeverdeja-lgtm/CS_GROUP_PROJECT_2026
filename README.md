EASY INVESTING

For our CS Project 2026, we decided to build a beginner-friendly website designed to make investing accessible to everyone. 
With this website, our goal is to make investing as simple as possible, such that even beginners who have never invested a single dollar can understand the basics and start investing. 

Our Website is organized around five interconnected pages, each serving a specific purpose in your investment journey.

Basics is your foundation. This educational guide walks you through core investing concepts—compound interest, risk levels, and diversification—all explained in plain language with concrete examples. It's everything a beginner needs to understand before diving deeper.

Profile helps you know yourself as an investor. A quick 7-question questionnaire assesses your risk tolerance by scoring your answers from 1 to 4 points. The higher your total, the more risk you're comfortable with. This becomes your north star for all other decisions on the platform.

Portfolio Allocation lets you build your own portfolio by searching and adding stocks one by one. You'll instantly see how your holdings are balanced across currencies, regions, industries, and risk levels. Each stock is labeled as low, medium, or high risk using real Yahoo Finance beta data, so you can spot concentration problems at a glance.

Stock Analyzer gives you the research tools to evaluate any stock in seconds. Compare up to 4 stocks side by side, dive into key metrics and charts, and see a 1-year price forecast. No jargon, just actionable insights.

News keeps you grounded in what's actually happening. Get a daily snapshot of market movers, a financial news feed, and live indices from 5 regions. Switch between 1 week, 1 month, and 1 year views to see trends at any timescale.
